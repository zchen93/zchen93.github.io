I am an Antenna Engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. My days are defined by VNA, MATLAB, and—on occasion—a camera, or a backpack. 

Behind every strategic decision and every frame I capture is a simple desire to understand the "why"—of electromagnetism, of mother nature, and of life’s purpose. I am an observer by nature, finding as much value in the silence of a high-altitude peak as in the complexity of human connection.

I am lucky enough to walk a path that balances a sense of duty with a love for the sublime. I don’t seek the center of the stage; I prefer the periphery, where the light is better and the conversations are deeper.

This site documents my technical builds and visual journey. Feel free to shoot me an email to discuss anything.
