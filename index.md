I am an RF engineer based near San Jose, California, and I sometimes take pictures. Behind every photo and every strategic decision is a simple desire to understand the "why." I am an observer by nature, finding as much value in the silence of a high-altitude peak as in the complexity of human connection.

I am lucky enough to walk a path that combines a sense of duty with a love for the sublime. I don't seek the center of the stage; I prefer the periphery, where the light is better and the conversations are deeper.
